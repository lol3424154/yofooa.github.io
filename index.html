Welcome

Welcome to PolyRacing! Get ready to experience the thrill of high-speed racing like never before. Our game features stunning graphics and animations, with a unique poly style that sets it apart from other racing games. You'll feel like you're racing through a futuristic world filled with bright colors and sharp angles.

- About Us

At PolyRacing, we're passionate about creating fun and exciting games that people love to play. Our team of talented developers and designers work tirelessly to create unique and engaging gaming experiences that stand out in the crowded world of video games.

Features

Play

In addition to single-player races, PolyRacing also features online multiplayer, where you can compete against other players from around the world.

Leaderboard

Reach #1 on the Leaderboard which you can see in the Game and in our Discord Channel, Join the ranks of the best racers in the world and start climbing the Leaderboard.

Contact

At PolyRacing, we believe in providing the best possible experience for our players. That's why we are always available to assist you questions at help@PolyRacing.com

Feedback

If you have any thoughts, ideas or found a bug in the game that you would like to share with us, we would love to hear from you! The bug finders, email bugs to help@PolyRacing.com

Chat

The in-game chat allows you to communicate with other players in real-time while you're racing. You can share tips and tricks, coordinate strategies.

Support

Our support team is available to assist you with any issues you may be experiencing, and our development team is always eager to hear your feedback and suggestions.
